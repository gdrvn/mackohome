My Story

James began his career in property valuation at Knight Frank before founding his independent practice in 2006. His boutique approach — handling a select number of clients at any one time — ensures every buyer and seller receives truly dedicated, personalised service.

Specialising in prime central London, James has an intimate knowledge of neighbourhoods from Belgravia and Kensington to Chelsea and Notting Hill. He has navigated clients through every kind of market — boom, correction, and recovery — always with calm expertise and clear guidance.

Discretion is paramount. Many of James's clients are repeat buyers, investors, or international families relocating to London. Confidentiality and trust are the foundation of every relationship he builds.

When not working, James is actively involved in local heritage preservation and sits on the board of the Kensington Residents' Association.
